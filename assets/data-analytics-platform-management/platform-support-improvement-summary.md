# Platform Support Improvement Summary

## Overview

This evidence summary documents workplace-aligned support process improvement across Qlik and Tableau reporting platforms.

The work focused on making platform administration more consistent, repeatable, and easier to support by improving documentation, access handling, licence visibility, and operational guidance.

All information in this document is sanitised, recreated, or summarised. No confidential organisational data is included.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this evidence is to demonstrate practical experience in improving platform support processes, including:

- Documenting repeatable support workflows
- Improving access request handling
- Supporting joiner, mover, and leaver processes
- Improving visibility of platform access and licences
- Reducing reliance on informal knowledge
- Helping make support activity more consistent
- Creating reusable templates and guidance

## IAM and Security Relevance

Support process improvement is relevant to IAM and access governance because inconsistent support processes can lead to inappropriate access, missed removals, poor licence visibility, and unclear accountability.

Improving platform support processes supports:

- More consistent access handling
- Better joiner / mover / leaver control
- Reduced stale access risk
- Improved licence governance
- Clearer operational ownership
- Stronger access review readiness
- Safer evidence capture
